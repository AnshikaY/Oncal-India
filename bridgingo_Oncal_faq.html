About us (in general)


What is Oncal India?

We are an entirely online market place where one can buy and sell as per their convenience and without having to leave the comfort of their home. We promote local trading of products and hence pair up sellers and buyers of the same region. For further details, visit About.

How did you get started?

A group of like minded people originally working for betterment of selling and buying conditions at their own levels came together to broaden the field of work with the belief of together we can.

How can I work at your organisation?

We’re looking for the best, brightest and most passionate people on the planet. Contact Us.

What is your refund/cancellation policy?

If for any reason you would like a refund for any product or service availed, we will ask the seller if they are willing to exchange/take back their product. After that it may take up to 10 business days (depending on your bank) for a refund to appear on your account. Vendors can cancel their subscription anytime by contacting us.
